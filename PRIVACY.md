# Privacy Policy for Mimir

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## Overview

Mimir is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how Mimir handles information when you use our application.

## Information Collection and Use

**Mimir does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.**

### What Mimir Accesses

Mimir requires the following system permissions to function:

- **Audio Access**: Mimir needs access to your system audio to detect running applications with audio output and control their individual volume levels.
- **System Events**: Mimir monitors which applications are producing audio to display them in the volume control interface.

All audio processing and volume control happens entirely on your local device. No information leaves your computer.

### What Mimir Does NOT Do

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track user behavior or usage patterns
- Does not store any data on external servers
- Does not share information with third parties
- Does not use analytics or tracking tools
- Does not display advertisements
- Does not require an account or login

## Data Storage

Mimir may store user preferences (such as preferred volume levels for specific applications) locally on your device using standard macOS system preferences. This data:

- Remains entirely on your device
- Is never transmitted to external servers
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the application

## Third-Party Services

Mimir does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

Mimir does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any users.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated revision date.
