## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I use Mimir?
Mimir runs in your menu bar and allows you to control the volume of individual applications independently. Click the menu bar icon to see all running audio applications and adjust their volumes.

### Why does Mimir need audio permissions?
Mimir requires audio access permissions to detect and control the volume levels of individual applications on your Mac.

### Is my data collected or shared?
No. Mimir does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All volume controls happen locally on your device. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for more details.

### What macOS versions are supported?
Mimir requires macOS [minimum version] or later.

### How do I uninstall Mimir?
Simply drag the Mimir app from your Applications folder to the Trash.
